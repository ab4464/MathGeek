Basics of Probability

Even if you've never taken a Probability course before, you'd agree that we all have some basic knowledge about probability. Indeed, we use the term frequently to make estimations in our daily life. Expression like: Do you know the probability that it rains today? and What are the chances that Mr. X is elected president? make reference to the probability of certain outcome.

Therefore, whenever we are not sure about the outcome of a specific event, we use probabilities to estimate the likelihood of its occurence. We calculate the probability of certain event by measuring the ratio of the favorable cases to the whole number of cases possible.
